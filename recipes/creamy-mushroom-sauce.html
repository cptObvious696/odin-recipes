Creamy Mushroom Sauce

Description

This creamy mushroom sauce is a delicious and versatile accompaniment to a variety of dishes. Made with fresh mushrooms, garlic, and cream, it adds a rich and savory flavor to pasta, chicken, steak, or even vegetables. The sauce is easy to prepare and can be customized with herbs and spices to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • 300g button mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 300ml heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat the butter and olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced mushrooms and cook until they release their moisture and begin to brown, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Stir in the garlic with salt & pepper and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the white wine and cook until it has mostly evaporated, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and chicken stock.
  6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  7. Add the dried thyme and stir to combine.
