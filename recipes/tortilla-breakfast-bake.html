Tortilla Breakfast Bake

Description

For this tortilla breakfast bake, accordion-folded tortillas hold thinly-sliced smoked sausage, followed by green onion, bell pepper, and cheese. Soaked in whisked eggs and half-and-half and baked, the casserole is rich, delicious, and spicy.

Ingredients

  • 10 (8 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 pound smoked sausage, thinly sliced
  • 1 bunch green onions, divided
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
  • 6 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup half-and-half
  • 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce, or to taste, plus more for serving if desired
  • avocado, salsa, or sour cream, for serving (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 baking dish.
  2. Fold each tortilla back and forth with 1 1/2 -inch folds like an accordion. Place each folded tortilla pressed against each other in the baking dish and lightly spread them apart until they fit the length of the dish.
  3. Place thinly sliced smoked sausage in the folds of the tortillas evenly throughout.
  4. Reserve 2 tablespoons green onions for garnish and sprinkle remaining green onions evenly over tortillas. Sprinkle with bell peppers and cheese.
  5. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, half and half, salt, pepper and hot sauce until well blended. Pour mixture over the casserole and use a fork or spatula to work the egg mixture within the tortilla folds.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until browned and bubbly, about 35 minutes. Serve topped with reserved green onions, more hot sauce, or with desired toppings.
